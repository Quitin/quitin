Storm Summoner Level Editor

Easy level editor for Storm Summoner.

Editor made by Quitin.
Storm Summoner was made by a team of Quitin (me), Watglan (WoodStuff) and Vovka11.
This game is a submission for the game jam. More specifically, 'The 1st Annual Meta Studio Summer Jam', or 'Flam Jam' in short.

Instructions

Specify the world size in the form '[width] [height]'.
For example, type '20 15' then press enter to make a world which is 20 blocks long and 15 blocks high.

Press keys 1-6 to switch between blocks.
1 - Empty block
2 - Solid block
3 - Checkpoint
4 - Spike
5 - Spawn (you must place only 1 per level)
6 - Goal (you must place only 1 per level)

Now you can start building the level!
The world needs a spawn and goal. Place only one of each!
After you're done, click 'Export' to generate a text file of your level.
To copy the level, click the orange area, do CTRL+A, then CTRL+C.
Go into Storm Summoner, press L to open up its own editor and paste (CTRL+V) your level into the textbox!
You can now play your own level. Have fun!
